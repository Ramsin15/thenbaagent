RAMSIN JACOB
LICENSED NBA PLAYER AGENT

Ramsin Jacob is a Licensed NBA Agent via National Basketball Player's Association.

Aside from the NBA, Ramsin has been a Licensed Real Estate Advisor for nearly a decade. Specializing in investment properties such as fix-and-flips, developments, and commercial ventures, he operates across Silicon Valley, the broader Bay Area, Southern California. In addition, catering to commercial institutional clientele beyond the state borders. With over 15 years of experience as a serial entrepreneur and a solid educational background, Ramsin holds a B.S. in Business Administration and three associate degrees in Marketing Management, Liberal Arts, and Business.

Recognizing the pivotal role of relationships in both life and business, Ramsin has successfully closed nearly $100,000,000 in real estate transactions. His expertise spans contracts, legal intricacies, management, marketing, and business, making him a trusted advisor for his clients. Armed with expert negotiation skills and comprehensive knowledge in acquisitions, risk assessment, financing, construction, and the disposition of various property types.

Having lived in diverse locations across the United States during different stages of his life, including Chicago, Hawaii, Santa Barbara, Santa Monica, and San Jose, Ramsin brings a well-rounded perspective to his endeavors. His early passion for sports instilled in him a relentless and tenacious approach to life and business.
