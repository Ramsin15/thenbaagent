Physical Profile & Athleticism

Sturdy, physically mature big with a strong, broad frame suited for PF or small‑ball C roles. Not an explosive leaper, but compensates with strength, balance, positioning, and a relentless motor.

Skill Set Breakdown — Rebounding

Tenacious rebounder, especially on the offensive glass. Uses strength, seals, and effort to create second‑chance opportunities. Track record of productivity at every level.

Skill Set Breakdown — Defense

Physical presence in the paint. Rotates well, contests at the rim, absorbs contact. Capable shot‑blocker with event creation through hustle. Can guard multiple front‑court positions.

Skill Set Breakdown — Offense

Thrives on effort plays: putbacks, rim runs, rolls, finishes through contact. Not a primary scorer but can hit open mid‑range and flashes situational 3‑pt range.

Intangibles

Resilient, team‑first leader. Overcame health adversity; respected by teammates and coaches for professionalism and work ethic. Vocal on court; leads by example.

Player History & Notable Background

Began in Grand Rapids, MI; standout at Wayland Union HS. Started at Rider; transferred to Northern Illinois, becoming a consistent starter and front‑court anchor. A high‑school health scare led to discovery of hypertrophic cardiomyopathy; cleared to play with monitoring—shaped his perspective and drive. Professionally, has played in the NBA G League (Windy City Bulls, Raptors 905, Grand Rapids, Rip City) and the British Basketball League (Surrey Scorchers).

Projection & Fit

Projects as a rotation big for teams seeking rebounding, defense, and toughness. Fits as a reliable G League rotation/spot starter or international starter/rotation piece. Continued growth in shooting consistency and foul discipline will further raise value.
